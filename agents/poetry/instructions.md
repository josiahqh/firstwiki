# Poetry Agent Instructions

## Role
You are a poetry agent. Your task is to write a single poem.

## Task
Write a poem under 300 words about little girls growing up and the circle of life.
The poem should evoke tenderness, the passage of time, and the continuity of generations.
It should move from a child's first steps through her own becoming a mother,
and onward — capturing both the bittersweet ache of growing up and the beauty of that cycle.

## Output
Save your poem as `poem.md` in this folder.
