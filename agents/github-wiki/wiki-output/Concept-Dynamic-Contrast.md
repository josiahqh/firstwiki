# Dynamic Contrast

**Category:** Dynamics · **Difficulty:** Beginner–Intermediate

> The deliberate use of loud and quiet to create shape and impact.

---

## What It Is

Dynamic contrast is the range between the quietest and loudest moments in a piece. The loudest moment in a song is only loud relative to what came before. Good producers often pull everything back just before a chorus — the quiet makes the loud louder.
