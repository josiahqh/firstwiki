# Call and Response

**Category:** Form · **Difficulty:** Beginner

> A musical conversation: one phrase *asks*, another *answers*.

---

## What It Is

One voice (or instrument) plays a phrase that feels incomplete or questioning; another voice completes or "answers" it. It creates dialogue, means nobody has to play all the time, and creates space. It is one of the most fundamental structures in music — present in gospel, blues, African music, funk, and jazz.
