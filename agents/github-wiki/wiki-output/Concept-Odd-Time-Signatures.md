# Odd Time Signatures

**Category:** Rhythm · **Difficulty:** Intermediate–Advanced

> Metres other than 4/4 and 3/4 — 5/4, 7/8, 6/8, and beyond.

---

## What It Is

| Time sig | Natural grouping | Example |
|----------|-----------------|---------|
| 5/4 | 3+2 or 2+3 | Take Five — Brubeck |
| 7/8 | 3+2+2 or 2+2+3 | Money — Pink Floyd |
| 6/8 | 2 groups of 3 (compound duple) | House of the Rising Sun |
| 9/8 | 3 groups of 3 | Blue Rondo a la Turk — Brubeck |

The challenge isn't counting — it's *feeling* the groove without counting.
