# 🎸 Jam Wiki

Welcome to the Jam Wiki — the shared memory and planning tool for our work-based music jam group.

---

## Our Two Goals

| | Goal | What it means in practice |
|-|------|--------------------------|
| 🎵 | **Bond over music** | A regular creative outlet — the joy is in playing together |
| 📚 | **Build musical literacy** | Every song and session is a chance to understand music more deeply |

We are approximately five members. Sessions involve live mixing of mic, MIDI, USB, acoustic, and plugged-in instruments.

## How the Wiki Works

The wiki is organised into six sections: **Repertoire**, **Sessions**, **Members**, **Concepts**, **Reference**, and **Resources**.

All five members can contribute — edit pages directly in the browser or clone the wiki repo and push. A maintainer reviews significant changes before they go live.
