## Overview

The iconic 1972 funk masterpiece, built around one of the most recognisable clavinet riffs in history. Chosen because it puts syncopation right at the centre of every bar and is immediately recognisable.

## Musical Focus

**Primary concept:** [[Concept-Syncopation|Syncopation]]  
**Secondary concept:** [[Concept-Groove-Pocket|Groove & Pocket]]

The clavinet riff lands almost entirely *between* the beats. The magic is that the drums and bass anchor the grid while everything else dances around it.

**What to listen for:**
- The ghost notes on the snare
- How the clavinet riff starts *before* beat 1
- Where the bass locks with the kick vs. where it diverges

**Further listening:** Stevie Wonder — *Innervisions*; James Brown — *Sex Machine*
