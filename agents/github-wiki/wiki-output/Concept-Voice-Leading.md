# Voice Leading

**Category:** Harmony · **Difficulty:** Intermediate–Advanced

> Moving each note of a chord to the nearest note of the next chord — smooth harmonic motion.

---

## What It Is

Good voice leading means individual notes move as little as possible when chords change. The result is that chord changes feel smooth and inevitable rather than lurching. This is why jazz pianists voice chords so differently from a beginner — they're keeping common tones in place and moving only what needs to move.
