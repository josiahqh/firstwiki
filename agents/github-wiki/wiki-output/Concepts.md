# Concepts

A reference library of musical ideas the group is exploring. Every song in the repertoire links to at least one concept; every concept page lists back the songs that cover it.

## Rhythm

| Concept | Summary |
|---------|---------|
| [[Concept-Syncopation\|Syncopation]] | Notes landing between the beats rather than on them |
| [[Concept-Groove-Pocket\|Groove & Pocket]] | The collective sense of rhythmic lock between players |
| [[Concept-Odd-Time-Signatures\|Odd Time Signatures]] | Metres other than 4/4 and 3/4 — 5/4, 7/8, 6/8, and beyond |
