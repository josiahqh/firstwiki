# Groove & Pocket

**Category:** Rhythm · **Difficulty:** Intermediate

> The collective sense of rhythmic lock between players — when the band breathes as one.

---

## What It Is

"Pocket" is the sweet spot of timing where a rhythm section sits together — not mechanically on the grid, but with a shared *feel*. A band in the pocket sounds inevitable. "Groove" is the forward momentum created by the interaction of rhythmic parts — it makes you want to move.

## Key Elements

- **Bass and kick drum alignment** — are they landing together, or fighting?
- **Consistent subdivision** — everyone is feeling the same internal pulse
- **Space** — playing in the pocket means knowing when *not* to play
- **Velocity control** — the same note hit harder or softer feels completely different
